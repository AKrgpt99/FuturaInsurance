Home Sweet Home

An investment into a home is one of the largest financial decisions you can make, so it's important to protect it against unforeseeable risks.

As a homeowner, you need to insure your house for its rebuilding/renovating costs in the event of serious damage or destruction. You should know who and what is covered, understanding the contractual obligations in your policy. Home insurance coverage includes personal liability coverage.

Coverage

The basic coverages you need for your home are: Fire, Theft & Vandalism, and Weather Damage.


Condominiums and Strata Corporations

Condominium and Strata Corporation policies are separate: one for a unit owner and another for the condominium or strata corporation.


Owner-Occupied Homes versus Homes For Rent

Insurance companies separate owner-occupied homes from homes that are rented to third party tenants. Changes in occupancy can affect your coverage, so always speak to your insurance representative if you are considering a change.

Landlords and tenants have different rights and responsibilities. Consider your insurance coverage and responsibilities before you rent your property.
