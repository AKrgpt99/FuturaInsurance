There are many different types of life insurance to choose from:

Term Life Insurance

Term life insurance provides a fixed amount of insurance over a certain time period. It is affordable, and the simplest type of life insurance.4


Whole Life Insurance

Also called permanent life insurance since it covers you permanently. This kind of insurance can also build in value over time, allowing you to save more money. With this insurance, it is guaranteed that your premium won't increase from the time you purchase the policy. These can also be similar to term life, where you only pay for a certain amount of time, after which you don’t have to pay anymore.


Universal Life Insurance

This is similar to a whole life insurance policy, combining protection and savings.5 You are guaranteed that your beneficiaries will receive the death benefit of your choosing.


Participating Life Insurance

This is another kind of whole life insurance. Just like whole life, your premium is guaranteed from the time of purchase. The difference is that you can receive dividends. You can cash out these dividends, or use them to reduce annual premiums, earn interest, or get more coverage.6
